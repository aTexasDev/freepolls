Frequently Asked Questions

Is this poll maker really free?

Yes! FreePollsApp is 100% free to use. Create unlimited polls with no signup required. For advanced features like analytics and unlimited votes, upgrade through Poll Pixie.

Do I need to create an account?

No account required for basic polls! Just type your question, add options, and share the link. Sign up for Poll Pixie to save and manage your polls.

Are the polls anonymous?

Yes, all votes are anonymous by default. Voters don't need to sign up or provide any personal information.

Can I see results in real-time?

Absolutely! Poll results update in real-time as votes come in.

What's the difference between FreePollsApp and Poll Pixie?

FreePollsApp is the fast, free poll creator. Poll Pixie is the full platform with analytics, custom branding, unlimited votes, and team features.
